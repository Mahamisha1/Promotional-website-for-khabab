Our Story

Discover the tradition behind our flavors

Since 1985: A Family Tradition

Markaza Kabab Arat was founded by Arat Mohammed in 1985, bringing authentic Middle Eastern flavors to our community. What started as a small family-run eatery has grown into a beloved culinary destination.

Our secret lies in the traditional recipes passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques.

Today, we continue this legacy with the same passion and commitment to quality that has defined us for over three decades.

About The Founder

Get to know the visionary behind Markaza Kabab Arat

Birthplace

Arat Mohammed was born in the historic city of Aleppo, Syria, where he first learned the art of Middle Eastern cooking from his grandmother.

Age & Experience

At 68 years young, Arat brings over 50 years of culinary expertise to every dish we serve at Markaza Kabab Arat.

Philosophy

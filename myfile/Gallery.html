Our Story

Discover the tradition behind our Islamic learning center

Preserving Islamic Knowledge

Markaza Kabab Arati was established in 2005 with the mission to provide authentic Islamic education to the community. Our center is dedicated to preserving and transmitting the rich Islamic tradition to future generations.

We offer comprehensive programs that cover Quranic studies, Hadith, Islamic jurisprudence, and spiritual development. Our curriculum is designed to cater to students of all ages and backgrounds.

With qualified scholars and teachers, we strive to create an environment that nurtures both knowledge and character development according to Islamic principles.
